The Famous Chicken Adobo

Photo by Frances Smith

Experience the all famous adobo of the Philippines. With the mixtures of condiments, you will be able to make the savoury and mouth-watering cuisine of the Philippines.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1(3 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, quartered and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2/3 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1.3 cup white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook chicken pieces until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
    Transfer chicken to a plate and set aside.
  2. Add onion and garlic to the skillet; cooko until softened and brown, and about 6 minutes. Pour in soy sauce and vinegar and season with garlic powder, black perpper, and bay leaf.
  3. Return chicken to pan, increase heat to high, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until chicken is tender and cooked through, 35 to 40 minutes.
